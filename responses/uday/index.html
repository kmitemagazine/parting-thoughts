Advice to 1st, 2nd, 3rd Year students
Now that’s a question where I should be answering responsibly I guess.. But you know what.. Let me tell you this… May be the most important thing is “Learn to FAIL “. Because engineering is the last safest thing that will happen to you guys.. If you fail now… You’ll have the support of your parents. May be your friends too. And trust me, you really don’t have to answer anyone…it’s your life Live it… live it right now..!!! Learn to study in a better way if you fail once. Never give up on getting up and fighting for what you deserve.. Sometimes its really hard for you to get back but this is just engineering phase of yours.. Trust me its a safe house… You will have hope as long as you believe in it . So never stop believing and never stop dreaming and never stop failing because once you’re out of this life… It’s really going to be hard for you to get back if you FAIL… Success is nothing but a lesson learnt from all your failures. So as long as you strive on learning new things everyday..You will definitely succeed one day
